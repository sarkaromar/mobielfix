The Phone Repair Specialists

iSmash specialises in offering an express repair service for smartphones, tablets and computers along with a wide range of mobile accessories and refurbished devices. Since launching in 2013, iSmash has grown to 20 repair shops across the UK and counting! We pride ourselves on offering an express repair service powered by our iSmash accredited technicians, on-site at each of our shops, and our screen repairs are backed by a lifetime warranty.
